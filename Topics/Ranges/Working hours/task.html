Working hours

The car wash works from 9 to 18 (we use a 24 hour time format) and employees want to replenish their strength with lunch for further work with 13 to 14.
As input, our program reads an integer, a positive number from the console, which is the desired time to write to the car wash and checks whether the entered time is within the working time.

If the time within working range hours returns true, but if the time is during lunch or outside working hours returns false.


Sample Input:
10

Sample Output:
true


Sample Input:
23

Sample Output:
false



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
